## [AI-Generated Fake Black Personas Used to Sell Shein on TikTok](https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence/938844/ai-tiktok-shop-blackface-shein-dropshipping) ⭐️ 6.0/10

The Verge reports that AI-generated fake Black personas are being used on TikTok to promote Shein products, exploiting racial representation for dropshipping profits. This practice raises serious ethical concerns about AI-generated deepfakes in advertising, particularly regarding racial exploitation and consumer deception. The article highlights a specific case where a fake Black woman persona named Aliyah was created to sell handmade metal buckles, crying on camera to gain sympathy and views.

rss · The Verge · May 30, 13:00

**Background**: AI-generated fake personas, also known as deepfakes, use machine learning to create realistic but entirely fabricated images or videos. These can be used for various purposes, including fraud and misleading advertising. The ethical implications are significant, as they can manipulate audiences and exploit identities without consent.
